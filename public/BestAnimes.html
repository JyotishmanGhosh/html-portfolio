The Best Animes According to Bobby

My top 3 Animes of all-time.

Demon Slayer

Popular due to its stunning animation quality by Studio Ufotable, compelling character development

Jujutshu Kaisen

Due to its captivating world-building with unique lore about curses, engaging action-packed fight scenes, complex and well-developed characters

Solo Leveling

Due to its combination of a compelling power fantasy story with a strong underdog protagonist, stunning artwork, exceptionally well-animated action sequences
